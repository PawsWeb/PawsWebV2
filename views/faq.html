What is PawsWeb?
PawsWeb is an online platform designed to raise awareness about the plight of homeless pets and to promote pet adoption. It allows users to browse pet profiles, schedule virtual meet-and-greets, and access educational resources.
How do I adopt a pet?
To adopt a pet, you can browse through the available pet profiles on our website. Once you find a pet you are interested in, you can schedule a virtual meet-and-greet through the website. After the meeting, if you wish to proceed, you can contact the shelter directly to complete the adoption process.
How do I schedule a virtual meet-and-greet with a pet?
To schedule a virtual meet-and-greet, navigate to the pet's profile page and click on the 'Schedule Meet-and-Greet' button. You'll be prompted to fill out a form with your details and preferred meeting time. Once submitted, you will receive a confirmation email with further instructions.
What should I do if I encounter a technical issue on the website?
If you encounter any technical issues while using PawsWeb, please contact our support team at PawsWeb@gmail.com. We'll do our best to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
